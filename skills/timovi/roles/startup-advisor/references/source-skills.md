# Source Skills Inventory

The Startup Advisor draws from these 8 source skills. To access a specific framework, invoke the named skill or read the relevant chapter file directly.

## Skills & Their Domains

| Skill | Domain | When to Consult | Key Frameworks |
|-------|--------|-----------------|----------------|
| `ries-lean-startup` | Lean Startup methodology | MVP design, pivot decisions, validated learning, innovation accounting | Build-Measure-Learn, MVP types, 3 engines of growth, 10 pivot types |
| `fitzpatrick-mom-test` | Customer discovery | Customer interviews, problem validation, avoiding false positives | 3 rules, commitment scale, bad data catalog, question taxonomy |
| `olsen-lean-product-playbook` | Product development | PMF, personas, value propositions, MVP scoping, prototyping | 6-step process, PMF Pyramid, importance-satisfaction, ICE |
| `gerber-e-myth` | Business systematization | Building systems, delegation, working ON vs IN, franchise prototype | 3 personalities, 3 phases, I-Q-O, 7-step program, Position Contracts |
| `ellis-brown-hacking-growth` | Growth | Growth teams, high-tempo testing, funnel optimization | Growth cycle (Analyze→Ideate→Prioritize→Test), ICE, must-have test |
| `collins-good-to-great` | Organizational excellence | Leadership, culture, strategic focus, flywheel | Level 5 leadership, Hedgehog Concept, Stockdale Paradox |
| `mckeown-essentialism` | Focus & prioritization | Saying no, eliminating non-essentials, executing on what matters | 90% rule, Explore/Eliminate/Execute, graceful no |
| `founders-playbook` | AI-native startups | AI-era lifecycle, founder as orchestrator, Claude tooling | 4-stage lifecycle, 3 AI pillars, Claude surface selection |
